How RayLearn Works

1. Power Independence

RayLearn is powered by solar energy with battery backup, ensuring students can study anytime — day or night — without relying on unstable electricity.

2. Offline Learning

A built-in tablet comes preloaded with curriculum-aligned lessons, videos, quizzes, and reading materials that work fully offline.

3. Safe Study Light

An integrated LED reading lamp replaces candles and kerosene lamps, improving safety and extending study hours.

4. Device Charging

USB ports allow students and families to charge phones or small devices, reducing the cost and time spent searching for power.

5. Exam-Focused Learning

RayLearn’s educational content is structured around key school subjects and exams, helping students revise important topics, practice questions, and improve academic performance even without internet access.

6. Smart Learning Design

RayLearn is designed to support focused and distraction-free learning. The system organizes lessons, practice questions, and revision materials in a simple interface that helps students stay on track, learn at their own pace, and build consistent study habits.
