Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 13, 2025

Overview

OctaLearn (octalearn.com.ng) is an open learning platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. Because OctaLearn is fully open (no user accounts), we do not require registration to use most features.

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit the site, including:

  • Device and browser information (user agent, screen size, OS)
  • IP address (for analytics and security)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time on page, clicks) via analytics tools

2. Cookies & Local Storage

OctaLearn uses cookies and local storage for:

  • Essential cookies: enable basic site functions and accessibility.
  • Functional cookies: store preferences and settings (e.g., theme, language).
  • AI personalization cookies: power client-side AI features like summary generation and local personalization. These may store temporary tokens or summary data locally to avoid server-side profiling.
  • Analytics cookies: aggregate usage data to help us improve the site.

You may manage cookie preferences from the cookie banner; blocking cookies may disable AI summary features.

How We Use Data

  • To operate and maintain the Service and improve content quality.
  • To enable AI features (client-side summaries) and store temporary local data needed to provide those features.
  • To analyze site usage and trends to enhance performance and user experience.
  • To protect the site from abuse and to detect security issues.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share aggregated, non-identifying analytics with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., analytics platforms or CDN providers). When we use third parties, we require that they provide appropriate safeguards.

Retention

We retain usage logs and aggregated analytics for as long as needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. Cookies and local storage items used for AI summaries are stored on the user's device and can be cleared by the user at any time.

International Transfers

OctaLearn is available to a global audience. Data (such as analytics logs) may be processed in countries other than your residence. Where required by law, we implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

Children

OctaLearn is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Cookies: You can accept or decline cookies via the cookie banner or browser settings.
  • Access & deletion: Because we do not create accounts, we generally cannot identify an individual user across devices. If you believe we hold personal data about you in server logs, contact us and we will assess reasonable requests.
  • Analytics opt-out: You may use browser extensions or privacy settings to opt out of certain analytics tracking.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no system is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to or from our Service.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will post the revised policy on this page with an updated "Effective date".
