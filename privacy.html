Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 13, 2025

Overview

OctaLearn (octalearn.com.ng) is an open learning platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address (for analytics and security)
  • Pages visited and interaction data

2. Cookies & Local Storage

  • Essential cookies for site functionality
  • Preference cookies (theme, settings)
  • AI personalization storage for summaries & tools
  • Analytics cookies to improve performance

How We Use Data

  • Operate and improve OctaLearn
  • Enable AI-powered learning tools
  • Understand user behavior and trends
  • Maintain platform security

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share anonymized analytics with trusted third-party services strictly for improvement and security purposes.

Data Retention

Cookies and AI-related data are stored locally on your device and can be cleared anytime. Server-side logs are retained only as long as necessary.

Children’s Privacy

OctaLearn is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

Your Rights

  • Accept or decline cookies
  • Clear stored data anytime via browser settings
  • Contact us for privacy-related concerns

Security

We implement reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.
